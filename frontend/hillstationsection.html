Hill Stations & Monsoon Trips

Matheran

Matheran, a unique hill station in Maharashtra, India, is known for being Asia's only automobile-free hill station and its stunning natural beauty, located in the Western Ghats at an elevation of 800 meters (2,625 feet). Matheran has many interesting viewpoints' each offering a unique perspective of the surroundings. Panorama Point, Echo Point, and Louisa Point are some of the most popular ones. These points allow visitors to witness the golden sunrises and sunset views of the Western Ghats!

Lonavala

Lonavala, a popular hill station in Maharashtra, is located in the Pune district, nestled in the Sahyadri range, approximately 64 km from Pune and 80 km from Mumbai, known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and serene lakes.

Khandala

This beautiful hill station is nestled at a height of 1,800 ft in the western ghats of Maharashtra. Khandala, a picturesque hill station nestled in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, is known for its serene environment, pleasant climate, historical significance and riveting vantage points. Some of the most popular tourist attractions include Bhushi Lake, Duke's Nose, Lion's Point and Rajmachi Fort.

Panchgani

Panchgani, a popular hill station in Maharashtra, offers stunning views, adventure activities, and a chance to relax amidst nature. Some must-see attractions include Table Land, Parsi Point, Sydney Point, and Mapro Garden, known for its strawberry products.

Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar, a popular hill station in Maharashtra, India, is known for its lush greenery, scenic beauty, and pleasant weather, and is located in the Western Ghats. It was once the summer capital of the Bombay Presidency and is a popular tourist destination.
