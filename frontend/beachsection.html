Beach & Coastal Getaway

Vengurla Beach

Vengurla Beach is a tranquil, pristine beach located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, near the Arabian Sea, known for its white sand, lush greenery, and calm waters, offering a peaceful escape for tourists.

Kihim Beach

Kihim Beach, a popular weekend getaway for those in Mumbai, is a small village located north of Alibag, accessible by road and water, and known for its unspoiled natural beauty and activities like horse cart rides and water sports.

Tarkarli Beach

Tarkarli Beach, a popular destination in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, is known for its serene coastline, clear waters, and opportunities for water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving, attracting tourists seeking a blend of sea and Konkan culture.

Shiroda Beach

Shiroda Beach is a tranquil, unspoiled beach located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, India, known for its white sand, calm waters, and proximity to the Terekhol and Mochemad rivers. There are several famous temples and religious sites near Shiroda beach. The Shri Sidheshwar Temple and other smaller religious spots serve as centers of faith for the local community.

Alibaug Beach

Alibaug Beach, a popular tourist destination in Maharashtra, India, is located in the coastal town of Alibaug, approximately 95 kilometers from Mumbai, known for its serene beauty and sandy shores. Alibag, also known as Alibaug (Pronunciation: [əlibaːɡ]), is a coastal city and a municipal council in Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It is the headquarters of the Raigad district and is south of the city of Mumbai.
