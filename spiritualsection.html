Spiritual & Temple Tour

Trimbakeshwar

It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. The extraordinary feature of the Jyotirlinga located here is the Linga in the temple is in the form of a three faced embodying Tridev, Lord Brahma, Lord Vishnu and Lord Shiva. All other Jyotirlingas have Shiva as the main deity.

Grishneshwar

Steeped in spiritual significance, the Grishneshwar Temple draws devotees from far and wide, seeking blessings and solace in the divine presence of the deity. As the smallest of the Jyotirlingas, it holds a special place in the hearts of believers, revered as the 12th and final Jyotirlinga of India.

Bhimashankar

Bhimashankar is famous as a revered pilgrimage site and a nature lover's haven, primarily for housing one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, and for its location in the lush Sahyadri hills of Maharashtra.

Shirdi (Sai Baba Mandir)

It is the former home to the spiritual guru, Sai Baba, whose mark is still imprinted with a Samadhi Mandir here. Thousands travel to the city each day to pay homage to the Sufi saint and seek blessings. The saint was known to preform miracles and spread teachings about 'One God.

Shegaon (Gajanan Maharaj Mandir)

Shri Gajanan Maharaj, a saint known for his miracles and deep spiritual wisdom, is believed to have appeared in Shegaon in the late 19th century. His divine presence and teachings continue to attract thousands of devotees who seek blessings, peace, and spiritual enlightenment.
